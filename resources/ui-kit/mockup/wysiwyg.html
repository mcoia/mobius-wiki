MOBIUS

This documentation supports Missouri's premier library consortium. MOBIUS provides shared resources and services to 72 member libraries across Missouri and neighboring states.

This section of the documentation contains links to external sites. Please be advised that these external sites are not maintained by MOBIUS and may be aligned with different MOBIUS releases or services.

Welcome to MOBIUS documentation. MOBIUS is a collaborative library consortium that provides access to shared collections, innovative technologies, and professional expertise. This open platform moves the library industry beyond traditional library management systems, offering libraries more choices of features and services.

A modern library ecosystem

The MOBIUS consortium aims to facilitate a sustainable, community-driven collaboration around the creation of modern technology infrastructure that empowers libraries through shared applications to manage library resources and expand library value.

Community collaboration

Development of MOBIUS services is driven by the transparent collaboration of librarians, developers, and vendors. A governance structure provides oversight of consortium development, with representation from the many member institutions and organizations involved.

History and Organization

MOBIUS was established in July 1998 by 50 libraries representing Missouri colleges and universities, with the University of Missouri acting as the initial host institution.

Technology Infrastructure

FOLIO Library Services Platform

MOBIUS has adopted the FOLIO Library Services Platform, hosted by EBSCO Information Services. FOLIO is an open-source, next-generation library management system that replaces traditional integrated library systems (ILS).

  • Circulation management – Check-in/check-out, holds, and renewals
  • Cataloging and metadata – MARC records and bibliographic control
  • Acquisitions and invoicing – Purchase orders and budget management
  • Electronic resource management – Licenses and access control

Services and Benefits

MOBIUS provides member libraries with comprehensive services designed to maximize resource access and operational efficiency:

  • Resource sharing – Direct borrowing and lending among member libraries
  • Courier service – Cost-effective physical delivery of materials
  • Professional development – Training opportunities and conferences
  • Technical support – Help desk services and system maintenance
